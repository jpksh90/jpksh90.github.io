About Me!

Hi, I am Jyoti. Currently, I am a PhD student at University of Passau in Chair of Software Engineering 1 with Christian Hammer. My current research interests are in static program analysis, information flow control, and polyglot programming. My PhD is focussed on static analysis of polyglot applications.

Prior, I graduated with a masters' degree in Computer Science from Saarland University in 2017 and a bachelors degree in Information Technology from VSSUT (India) in 2012. In addition, I have also worked in IBM Consulting as a software developer 2012 to 2014.
