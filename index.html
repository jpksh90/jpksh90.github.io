About Me!

Hi, I am Jyoti.

I am a PhD student at University of Passau in Chair of Software Engineering 1. My research is supervised by Prof. Dr-Ing. Christian Hammer. Before moving to Passau, I started as a PhD student with him at the University of Potsdam.

I graduated with a masters' degree in Computer Science from University of Saarland in 2017. Before that, I had spent my lovely four years of bachelors degree in VSSUT (Burla, India) where I graduated with a bachelors degree in Information Technology in 2012. I also worked in IBM Consulting as a software developer 2012 to 2014. I was born and raised in Bhubaneswar in India.
