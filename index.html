About Me!

Hi, I am Jyoti.

I am currently a PhD student at the University of Passau and currently affiliated with the Chair of Software Engineering 1 since Oct'2021. I work with Prof. Dr-Ing. Christian Hammer, who supervises my PhD. Before, moving to Passau, I started as a PhD student with Christian at the University of Potsdam.

I graduated with a masters' degree in Computer Science from University of Saarland in 2017. Before that, I spent my lovely four years of bachelors degree in VSSUT in Burla, India from where I graduated with a bachelors degree in Information Technology in 2012.

I have also worked in IBM Consulting as a software developer for around two years from 2012 to 2014. I was born and raised in Bhubaneswar in India.
